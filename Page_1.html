What exactly is Ethical Hacking?

 Ethical or "White-hat" hacking is the act of breaking, bypassing, and exploiting flaws in digital security on systems after receiving express permission from those being attacked. Every major company worth their salt employs such hackers to attempt to exploit their security. The main differences between this and what is known as malicious or "Black-hat" hacking is that permission is obtained—making it legal—and the hackers are hired by the ones they hack.
