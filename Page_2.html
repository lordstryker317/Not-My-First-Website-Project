How does this benefit me?

 The best way to explain it is with an analogy. Say you are tasked with protecting a bank from robberies. You can try your best but there might be some things you missed. As such you might hire a robber to try to break in. He could then tell you what he did to get past you and you could try to fix the problem. This would ensure that any subsequent robbers who were real wouldn't be able to get in the same way.
 This is the same model used for cybersecurity. Because the cyber space is so abstract it can be very difficult for cybersecurity professionals to anticipate the actions of malicious actors. This is where ethical hacking comes in.
 Ethical hackers can attempt to find chinks in the armor of these systems and services in order to develop defenses prior to an attack. Without ethical hackers, your data would be protected by extremely flimsy security and could fall victim to any hacker with sufficiently advanced skills.
